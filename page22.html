About Us

Greenythrifthy was established in 2020 by a group of students studying at a university. This online store was established due to the fact that there were many surplus clothes and bags that were not being used, causing them to think of establishing a website where people could buy them there. Because this establishment got the attention of many students, some students also sold their clothes to Greenythrifty for resale. Known for selling relatively cheap clothing, Greenythrifty's success has been credited to its popularity among students. Greenythrifty sells a variety of clothes including abayas, blouses, hijabs and totebags.
